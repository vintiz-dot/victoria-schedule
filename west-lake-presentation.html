The Math Mandate — Phase 1
SCALING REALITY

AI gives you the ideas, but human logic makes them buildable. Establish the math on your A3 grid paper.

  • ①
    Define your scale (e.g., 1 square = ? metres) to fit the 400m × 400m plot on your paper.
  • ②
    Establish your scale factor k — the constant of proportionality.
  • ③
    Write the governing equation y = kx for your blueprint.
  • ④
    Calculate the drawing dimensions (x) to plot your structures accurately on the grid.
THE PROPORTIONALITY MODEL
y = kx
x Drawing dimension (cm or squares)
y Real-world dimension (metres)
k Scale factor (metres per square)
💡 EXAMPLE SCALE

If 1 grid square = 10m, then k = 10

A 50m structure → x = 50 ÷ 10 = 5 squares
