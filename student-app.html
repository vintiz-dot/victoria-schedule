  • 1
    Use the AI Co-Pilot tab to get realistic real-world dimensions (length × width in metres) for each of your 3 zones.
  • 2
    Choose your scale factor k so the entire 400 m × 400 m plot fits neatly on your A3 grid paper. (e.g. 1 square = 10 m → k = 10)
  • 3
    Write the governing equation: y = kx where x = drawing size (cm/squares) and y = real-world size (m).
  • 4
    Calculate the drawing dimension for each zone: x = Real Dimension ÷ k. Plot all 3 zones onto your A3 paper within the 400 m boundary.
  • 5
    Calculate the actual area of each zone: Area = Real Length × Real Width (in m²). Do NOT scale up areas!
